Director’s Message

Greetings from NITTTR, Chennai.

I am pleased to extend greetings to the readers of volume 5, issue 2 of the NITTTR, Chennai Newsletter. Since its establishment in 1964, the National Institute of Technical Teachers Training and Research (NITTTR), Chennai has provided high-quality teacher education by motivating, energizing and building teachers’ intellectual and social capacity.

The Institute’s academic and socio-cultural events in the second half of 2022 conceptualize a vibrant multidisciplinary institution supporting the Nation’s cause for self-reliance and holistic development. Encouraging teachers to rise above the challenges of virtual teaching- learning scenarios and providing counselling support for students struggling to continue their education are the commitments of NITTTR, Chennai. I am happy to be a part of this belief to transform our country’s teacher education landscape.

Prof. Dr. Usha Natesan Director, NITTTR, Chennai
